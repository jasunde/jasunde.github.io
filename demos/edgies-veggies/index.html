SALAD GREENS

Snows gone!

Just like that, I’m out prepping beds with my new broadfork, and learning how in-shape I am not. This brutal hunk of steel is paramount for farming ecologically on a small scale. The long tines gently lift, loosen and aerate deep underground while its operator, powered by water and PB&Js, quickly looses strength, energy and breath. Hours later, in the hot windy mess of an April afternoon, lettuces, turnips and spinach were the first to make it in the ground. Why do I go thru all this effort? Because, Lovelies, I am driven to nurture YOU with healthy plants from healthy soil.

Edgie will be back at market on Saturday with microgreens and shoots! Come say, hi, grab a baked yummy and enjoy the seasonal reprieve.
